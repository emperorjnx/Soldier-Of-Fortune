Welcome to the help section of SOF.

The help section will introduce you to some basic commands in the 

use          - uses the item with you to perform some action
front or up  - makes your character move forward
back or down - makes him move back
left         - makes him move left
right        - makes him move right
look         - makes him look around
exit         - exit the game
yes/no       - to enter your choices

You can press the 'q' button if you want to stop a video
Enjoy the game!!
